Common Questions

Why do you show your pricing publicly?
Because hiding prices wastes everyone's time and erodes trust. Retainers range from ₹50,000–₹1,00,000/month depending on which services you take — media buying alone is at the lower end; the full growth stack (strategy, creative, media buying, CRO, analytics and retention) at the upper end. A comparable in-house team costs ₹5–10L/month. We'll quote your exact number on the call based on what your brand actually needs.
What does "live results on the call" actually mean?
We share our screen and open real client Ads Manager accounts in front of you — live campaigns, today's spend, today's ROAS. Not screenshots, not slides. We do this because results that can't survive a live screen-share aren't results.
Why do you require ₹6,000+/day in ad spend?
Below that, there isn't enough data velocity to test creatives, exit learning phases and scale within 90 days — and our retainer wouldn't be a sensible investment for you. We'd rather turn you away today than take your money and underdeliver.
How is this different from other agencies?
Most agencies do media buying only — roughly 20% of the scaling problem. We operate the entire growth stack and take ownership of outcomes. Once you're a client, your account is run by senior strategists with weekly working sessions — not handed off to junior account managers. And we prove our results live before you pay a rupee.
I've been burned by agencies before. Why trust you?
Don't trust us — verify us. On the call we show live dashboards, walk through real case studies in your category, and can connect you with current clients to speak to directly. Make us pass your due diligence before you decide anything.
Do you lock clients into long contracts?
No. We work in 90-day sprints — the realistic window to build systems and see compounding results. After that, clients continue month-to-month. Retention comes from performance, not paperwork.
